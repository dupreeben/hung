Hi. I'm Ben.

I'm a fiction writer interested in literary fiction, mystery, Japan, the American west, dystopia, magical realism, and tanka poetry.

Currently, I'm in the final stages of editing my debut novel, Sarin Sunset, a literary mystery/bildungsroman set in and out of Tokyo, centered on two people whose lives were turned upside-down by the 1995 sarin gas attack.

My other occasional work is available on Short Scenes, my rough proving ground and repository.

In a past life, I worked as a communications strategist and copywriter for U.S. Senators, members of Congress, Mayors, non-profits, and candidates for public office across America.

I live in Portland, Oregon with my wife Mele. My joys include riding my road bike as fast as I can, frequenting good bars and coffee shops, staring wistfully into nature, my two cats, and obsessively rooting for the Los Angeles Dodgers and Lakers.

--Ben
