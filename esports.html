We are the Hidden Urban Ninja Guild. We are buddies who play games and build community.

Our team plays Playerunknown's Urban Battlegrounds (PUBG) competitively and is recruiting for other games.

Our captain is therottedzombie. Our other founding members are: ariesx, Lt. Dan's Leggz, seedmole, DangerLiveBees, jojoshsh, and DylStroy. Watch us play on Twitch. Get to know us better or get competing in our Discord.
